# Lead lifecycle
A lead is an inquiry / RFQ email that stays in **Leads** until it is completed and closed by Admin.

# Step 1 — Lead arrives
Leads are captured from: `leads@nexcelservice.net`

NexTraQ stores:
- Subject, sender, received date
- Original email as `.eml`
- Attachments (PDF, docs, images)

# Step 2 — Assignment
Admin / Head assigns an owner. The owner sees it under **My Leads**.

# Step 3 — Discovery notes
In **Lead Details**, fill **Discovery / Requirements Notes** (do not paste the forwarded email body here).

# Step 4 — Rep completion
When you send the outgoing quote:
- Enter **Outgoing Quote Reference**
- Add completion notes (optional)
- Submit for admin closure

# Step 5 — Admin closure
Admin verifies the outgoing quote and then closes the lead with closure notes.
